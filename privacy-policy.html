Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Finance Master ("Company," "we," "us," "our," or "Site"). Finance Master operates the finance education website located at financemaster.com (the "Site"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our Site.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and otherwise handle your information when you visit our Site, including any other media form, media channel, mobile website, or mobile application related or connected to it.

2. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide Directly

  • Registration & Account Data: When you create an account or contact us, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, and any other information you provide.
  • Communication Data: Messages you send us through contact forms, emails, or support channels.
  • Payment Information: If applicable, billing and payment details (processed securely through third-party payment processors).

B. Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser type, and unique device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, links clicked, and referring website.
  • Location Data: Approximate location based on IP address (not precise GPS).
  • Cookies & Similar Technologies: We use cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience and collect analytics.

C. Third-Party Data

We may receive information about you from third-party sources, including analytics providers and advertising partners.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Provide and improve our Site and educational content
  • Process your requests and respond to inquiries
  • Send educational emails, newsletters, and updates (with your consent)
  • Analyze user behavior and trends for Site optimization
  • Detect and prevent fraudulent activity and security issues
  • Personalize your experience and content recommendations
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Display targeted advertising through Google Ads and AdSense

4. Sharing Your Information

We do NOT sell or rent your personal data. However, we may share information in these circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who assist with analytics (Google Analytics), advertising (Google AdSense), hosting, and email services.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our legal rights.
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.
  • Aggregate Data: Anonymous, aggregated data may be shared for research and marketing purposes.

5. Google Analytics & Advertising

We use Google Analytics to understand how users interact with our Site. Google Analytics collects anonymized data about your visits. You can opt-out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

We also use Google AdSense and other advertising networks to display targeted ads. These partners may use cookies to serve ads based on your browsing history.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including SSL encryption, firewalls, and secure servers. However, no transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access & Correction: You can request access to or correction of your personal data by contacting us.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt-out of marketing emails by clicking the unsubscribe link in our emails.
  • Cookie Control: Most browsers allow you to control cookies through settings. Disabling cookies may affect Site functionality.
  • Do Not Track: Some browsers support "Do Not Track" signals. We do not respond to these signals at this time.

8. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our Site uses cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Session management and user preferences
  • Analytics and performance tracking
  • Personalized advertising and content

Third-party partners (Google, advertising networks) may also place cookies on your device. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Site is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete it immediately.

10. International Data Transfers

If you are located outside India, your data may be transferred to and stored in India. By using our Site, you consent to such transfers.

11. Retention of Information

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. You may request deletion of your data, subject to legal requirements.
